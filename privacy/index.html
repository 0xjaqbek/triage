Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

TRIAGE MCI ("the App") is a mass casualty incident (MCI) triage management tool developed by Jakub Skwierawski. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data entered into the App — including patient information, team names, hospital names, and event details — is stored exclusively on your device using the browser's localStorage.

2. Data Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device only.
  • No data is sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • No analytics, tracking, or telemetry is used.
  • No cookies are used for tracking purposes. The App uses only a service worker cache for offline functionality.

3. Personal Information

The App does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information. No names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other identifying information is collected by the App developer.

4. Patient Data

Patient data entered during triage (triage category, sex, age, notes) is stored only in your device's local storage. This data is never transmitted over the network. The user is solely responsible for the handling of patient data in accordance with applicable medical data protection regulations (e.g., GDPR, HIPAA).

5. SMS and Email Features

The App offers the ability to send reports via SMS or email. These features use your device's native SMS and email applications. The App itself does not send any messages directly — it only opens the respective native app with pre-filled content. The App has no access to your contacts, messages, or email accounts.

6. Internet Access

The App is designed to work fully offline as a Progressive Web App (PWA). Internet access is only needed for the initial download and subsequent updates. No data is transmitted during normal use.

7. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No SDKs from third parties are included.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is a professional medical tool intended for use by trained emergency medical personnel. It is not directed at children under 13. No data from children is knowingly collected.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Using the "End Event" function within the App, or
  • Clearing your browser's site data / localStorage, or
  • Uninstalling the App.

10. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
